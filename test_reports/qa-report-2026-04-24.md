# QA Report - 2026-04-24

Target: `http://127.0.0.1:3000`

Scope covered:
- Mobile navigation
- Settings / team-role management
- Invoice creation
- Invoice print layout
- Core sales / customer / product list flows

## Findings

### High

1. Invoice print page is not print-only and still renders the full app shell.
   - Repro:
     1. Sign in as an owner/admin user.
     2. Open any invoice.
     3. Click `Print` or navigate to `/sales/{invoiceId}/print`.
   - Actual:
     - The print page still includes the sidebar, header, search bar, and app chrome.
     - The printable document is embedded inside the application layout instead of being isolated as a clean print canvas.
   - Impact:
     - Printed/exported invoices are not presentation-ready and do not match the intended paper/PDF output.

2. Create Invoice allows saving a draft invoice with no valid line item data and a zero total.
   - Repro:
     1. Open `/sales/new`.
     2. Select a customer only.
     3. Do not choose a product and do not enter a rate.
     4. Click `Save Draft`.
   - Actual:
     - The app creates an invoice successfully.
     - The resulting invoice contains a blank item row with quantity `1`, rate `0.00`, amount `0.00`, and total `0.00`.
   - Impact:
     - Users can create invalid financial documents that look complete enough to be mistaken for real invoices.

### Medium

3. Invoice print header branding is visually broken even inside the invoice canvas.
   - Repro:
     1. Open `/sales/{invoiceId}/print`.
   - Actual:
     - The company name/logo block is vertically compressed and unreadable compared with the intended invoice sample.
   - Impact:
     - The invoice does not match the production-style document the business expects.

## What Worked

- Mobile dashboard layout rendered correctly at a phone viewport and the hamburger navigation opened and navigated successfully.
- Settings `Team & Roles` create-user flow worked.
- Role update worked for the created test user.
- User removal worked for the created test user.
- Sales list loaded and reflected newly created invoices.
- Customer list and product list data were available through the authenticated app session.
